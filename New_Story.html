WHO WE ARE

A Network of Specialized Schools with One Shared Purpose

New Story operates a network of over 75 schools and programs that provide individualized education, therapeutic support, and pathways for growth. We partner with districts to deliver the right services in the right setting— whether in one of our schools or within the district itself.

Our educators, clinicians, and staff share a common mission: to create learning environments where every student feels seen, supported, and capable of success.

Our Services

Meeting Students’ Needs—In Our Schools or In Yours

Our Schools

New Story Schools serve students with autism, emotional and behavioral disabilities, and mental health challenges. Each school offers small class sizes, clinical support, and a team-based approach to help students build academic, social, and emotional skills.

In-District Services

Through our In-District Services (IDS), we bring specialized programming, staff, and expertise directly into district schools—allowing students to stay closer to home while receiving the support they need.
